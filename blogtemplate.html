Stevens Institute Mentorship

Posted on October 5, 2015 by Olivia

Today we met with our mentors at Stevens Institute of Technology!

A few weeks ago, we contacted Dr. Salman of Stevens, and he arranged a meeting with us and the Stevens Institute Robotics Club.

At the meeting, we showed the club the FTC game reveal, and discussed the game in general. In addition, we showed them our CAD video from last year and our progress on the bot and code thus far.

They were very helpful in giving build and programming advice, as all of the members had competed in FTC, FRC, or VEX in high school.

We are looking forward to working with them more in the future!
